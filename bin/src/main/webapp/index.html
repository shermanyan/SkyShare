Welcome to SkyShare! Connecting Students with Reliable and Affordable Airport Rides At SkyShare, we understand the challenges of getting to and from the airport as a college student. Whether you are flying home for a break, traveling for a conference, or going to have fun in Cabo, we make your airport commute easy and economical. Join the SkyShare community today to share rides with fellow USC students. Save on travel, meet new friends, and enjoy the convenience of planning your trips with just a few clicks. Safety First: We prioritize your safety with verified profiles and easy communication directly through the app. Ready to simplify your travel plans? Download SkyShare now and start riding smart!

Get Started
  1. Sign Up: Create your free account using your college email.
  2. Find or Offer a Ride: Search for a ride on your date of travel or offer a seat in your car.
  3. Book & Share: Secure your seat through our app and coordinate with your ride companions.
  4. Travel Together: Meet up at your designated campus location and enjoy your ride to the airport.
